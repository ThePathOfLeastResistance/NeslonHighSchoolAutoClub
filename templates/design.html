Nelson High School Auto Club

The Front

For out main frame, we used 4 by 4 steal and welded it together. The ev is mostly made of 3 main parts, the front which has the steering and break system, the body, which is where the driver is located and the back, which is where most of the electronics is located

Break System

For our Break system, we used a hydratic system. On the left hand side of our car, is the master cylinder which is triggered by rotation system hooked up with the break peddel. The Master clyinder is connected to the 3 hydraulic break callipers, 2 for the front wheel and one for the back wheel, through Brake Flex hoses

Steering

For the stering system we used a track and pinion steeering system which is connected to a rode usiong a universal joints to the steering shaft. When the wheel is turned the shaft changes the wheels in the same direction

Body

The middle of our Ev is where the driver is situated, the chair was from a broken school chair, behind the driver's head is the head rest which was manufactored in nelson. Beside the driver is the emgancy breaks taht was taken from a broken car
