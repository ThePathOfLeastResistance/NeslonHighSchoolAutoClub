Get To Know The Team

The Nelson High School Electric Vehicle Challenge Team, comprising 15 dedicated students and three teachers, shares a vision of innovation and engineering excellence. Despite facing unexpected challenges in the previous year's competition, they have demonstrated resilience and a strong commitment to improving their electric vehicle. Previous achievements, including winning the Toyota Fabrication & Manufacturing Award, highlight their capabilities and commitment to success. They embody innovation, collaboration, and a passion for sustainable transportation, making them a formidable force in electric vehicle engineering.

The Electric Vehicle Challenge

The Waterloo High School Electric Vehicle Challenge at the University of Waterloo is an exciting competition where regional students design, build, and race electric cars in 12 and 24-volt classes. This event combines innovation, engineering, and sustainability, offering real-world learning and fostering a passion for clean energy and sustainable transportation.
